Mystory Ensō Support

Help with data, subscriptions, and app issues

To protect your privacy, do not send complete memory text, original private photos, Apple ID passwords, verification codes, or banking information in a support email.

On-device data

  • Export: Your Space > Privacy & Data > Export all on-device data.
  • Delete: choose Delete all on-device data on the same page and complete both confirmations.
  • Deleting app data or uninstalling does not automatically cancel an Apple subscription.

Ensō+ subscriptions

  • Restore: choose Restore Purchases on the Ensō+ screen.
  • View or cancel: choose Manage Subscription on the Ensō+ screen or use Subscriptions in your iPhone Apple Account settings.
  • Apple decides refund requests through its Report a Problem process.

Contact

For product and technical support email support@shide.app. For privacy, anonymous analytics deletion, or data-boundary questions email privacy@shide.app. Include the app version, iOS version, reproducible steps, and screenshots that do not reveal private content.

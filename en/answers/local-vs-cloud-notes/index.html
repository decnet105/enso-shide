A neutral comparison framework

How to choose between local-first notes, cloud journals, and knowledge-base tools

The right tool depends on your data and use case, not a single ranking. Family memories, team collaboration, and personal knowledge management place different demands on sync, privacy, and output.

Before comparing any specific product

Check the product's current official documentation, pricing page, and privacy policy, and record the date you verified them. Features change, so old reviews or user speculation should not be written up as permanent facts.

Ensō v1 is a hybrid architecture of “an account-free local core plus clearly disclosed networked services”; cross-device cloud collaboration is not part of its current public capabilities.
